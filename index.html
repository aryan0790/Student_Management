Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a student or admin?

Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the website, fill in your details, and select the appropriate role (student or admin) during registration.

How do I manage my events and academic information?

Once logged in, you can access your dashboard, where you will find sections for managing academic details, tracking attendance, and registering for events.

Can I change my role after registration?

No, roles cannot be changed once assigned during registration. If you have registered with the wrong role, please contact support for assistance.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login modal, and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

About Us

At StudentMS, we are committed to providing students and administrators with a seamless way to manage academic records, events, and other activities. Our platform is designed to simplify the management process for both students and admins, offering a centralized solution for tracking student progress, managing events, and accessing important academic information.

Our Vision
